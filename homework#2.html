THIS IS AN SIMPLE AND EASIEST WAY OF MAKING A MANGO ICE CREAM

  1. firstly you need a mango and half cup of milk and sugar
  2. then cut the mango into pieces
  3. then put the mango and milk in a grinder with 1 spoon of suger and grinde it
  4. now put the mixture in the refrigerator for 1.5 hour
  5. after that your mango ice cream should be ready to serve and eat
